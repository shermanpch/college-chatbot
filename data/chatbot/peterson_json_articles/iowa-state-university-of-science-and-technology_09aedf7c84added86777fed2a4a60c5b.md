### Iowa State University of Science and Technology

Iowa State University of Science and Technology, located in Ames, Iowa, is a public institution renowned for its strong focus on STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) disciplines. With a vibrant campus community and an undergraduate enrollment of over 25,000, Iowa State offers students a supportive environment to explore their academic and personal interests.

## Academics

Iowa State University provides a wide-ranging curriculum with over 100 undergraduate majors spanning diverse fields, from Agriculture and Engineering to the Arts and Humanities. Signature programs such as Agricultural Engineering, Environmental Science, and Bioinformatics are particularly well-regarded, reflecting the university's commitment to research and innovation. The student-faculty ratio of 18:1 allows for personalized attention, fostering an engaging learning atmosphere where students can thrive in discussion-heavy seminars and collaborative projects.

## Admissions

Iowa State is considered moderately selective, with an acceptance rate of 89%. Students typically present an average GPA of around 3.75, indicating a strong academic background. While standardized test scores are not explicitly required, the average SAT scores hover around 620 for reading and 630 for math. Early Decision and Regular admission options are available, making it flexible for applicants looking to secure their place early or during the regular cycle.

## Cost & Aid

The cost of attendance at Iowa State University falls into the moderate range, with tuition and fees being budget-friendly compared to many private institutions. Additionally, the university is committed to supporting students financially; on average, financial aid packages cover around 78% of demonstrated need. This can help make a college education more accessible, as approximately 98% of students receive some form of financial assistance, with the average aid package totaling about $14,829.

## Campus Life

Life at Iowa State is vibrant and diverse, with 31% of undergraduates residing in college-owned housing, which includes options for co-ed, men-only, and women-only living arrangements. Students engage in a variety of organizations, including Greek Life, intramural sports, and cultural clubs, creating a collaborative and inclusive community. Support services such as health clinics, counseling, and a women’s center enhance student well-being, ensuring that everyone has the resources they need to succeed both academically and personally.

## Athletics

Iowa State competes in NCAA Division I athletics, fostering school spirit and camaraderie among students and alumni. The Cyclones have a storied history in sports, particularly in basketball and football, where passionate fans create an electric atmosphere during home games. With a range of varsity teams and opportunities for intramural participation, there’s something for every sports enthusiast.
