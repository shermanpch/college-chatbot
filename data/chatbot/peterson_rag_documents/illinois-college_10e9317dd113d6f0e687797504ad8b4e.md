Illinois College is a private liberal arts institution located in Jacksonville, Illinois. With an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 940 students, this college prides itself on being student-centered and fostering a close-knit community that encourages personal and academic growth. Known for its strong emphasis on liberal arts education, Illinois College offers a vibrant environment that nurtures critical thinking and creativity.

## Academics
Illinois College provides a broad range of academic programs spanning from the sciences to the humanities. Students can choose from over 30 majors, including unique offerings such as Pre-veterinary Studies and Japanese Studies. The student-faculty ratio of 12:1 ensures that classes are intimate and discussion-focused, allowing students to engage deeply with their instructors and peers. This personalized attention can significantly enhance the learning experience, making it easier for students to explore their interests and achieve their academic goals.

## Admissions
Illinois College is highly accessible, boasting a remarkable acceptance rate of 100%. This means that all students who apply are admitted, provided they meet the basic requirements, including a high school transcript and an essay. While a minimum GPA of 2.5 is recommended, students with a solid academic background are encouraged to apply. Early decision applications are due by December 1, while notifications for accepted students are sent out shortly thereafter on December 23.

## Cost & Aid
The financial landscape at Illinois College is designed to be budget-friendly, with average tuition costs typically falling within a moderate range. The college is committed to making education affordable, as evidenced by its generous financial aid offerings, which include an average financial aid package of approximately $34,264. Furthermore, about 99% of students receive some form of financial assistance, which covers nearly 91% of their demonstrated financial need, making Illinois College an attractive option for many families.

## Campus Life
Campus life at Illinois College is characterized by a spirit of collaboration and community. While specific details about housing options were not reported, students typically find comfortable and supportive environments that foster friendships and personal growth. The college is known for its active student organizations and clubs, which provide numerous opportunities for involvement beyond the classroom. From cultural events to academic societies, students can easily find their niche and contribute to the vibrant campus community.

## Athletics
Illinois College competes in NCAA Division III athletics, offering a variety of sports for both men and women. Students can participate in varsity teams such as basketball, soccer, and swimming and diving, alongside numerous intramural sports options like badminton and racquetball. The college promotes a healthy lifestyle and sportsmanship, making athletics an integral part of campus life.
